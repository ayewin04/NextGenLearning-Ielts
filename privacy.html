Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

IELTS Myanmar ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our website.

Information We Collect

We do NOT collect any personal information. You can use our website without creating an account or providing any personal data.

However, we may collect anonymous usage data through:

  • Local Storage: We save your cookie preferences in your browser's local storage.
  • Browser Console: No personal data is stored or transmitted to our servers.

Cookies

Our website uses a single cookie to remember your cookie consent preference. This cookie is stored locally on your device and does not contain any personal information.

We do NOT use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or third-party analytics cookies.

Third-Party Services

Our website uses the following third-party services:

  • Font Awesome - for icons (CDN service). Their privacy policy: https://fontawesome.com/privacy
  • Browser Speech Synthesis API - for text-to-speech functionality. This works entirely in your browser and no data is sent to any server.

Children's Privacy

Our website is intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

Your Rights

Since we collect no personal data, there is no data to access, delete, or correct. You can:

  • Clear your browser's local storage at any time
  • Disable cookies in your browser settings
  • Use your browser's private/incognito mode
