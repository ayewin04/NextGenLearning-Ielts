Privacy Policy NextGenLearningMyanmar

📅 Last updated: 28 May 2026 · Effective immediately

Welcome to NextGenLearningMyanmar (the "Website", "we", "us", "our"). We provide a 15,000+ vocabulary learning platform with Burmese translations, audio pronunciation, and grammar resources — completely free. Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our website.

At a glance: We collect ZERO personal data — no logins, no tracking scripts, no third-party analytics. Your learning remains private.

1. Information We Collect

We do NOT collect any personally identifiable information (PII). You can access all vocabulary lists, search, listen to pronunciations, and use all features without creating an account, providing an email, or submitting any personal data.

However, like most static websites, anonymous technical data may be stored locally in your browser to improve functionality:

  • Local Storage: We save your cookie consent preference (so we don't show the cookie banner repeatedly). This is stored only on your device and never transmitted to our servers.
  • Browser console / network: We host zero tracking pixels or analytics scripts. No IP logging, no device fingerprinting.

2. Cookies & Local Storage

Our website uses a minimal cookie/local storage entry cookieConsent to remember if you've accepted the cookie notice. This is not a tracking cookie — it simply stores a true/false flag. You can clear it anytime via browser settings.

No advertising cookies, session cookies, or cross-site tracking cookies are used. We are 100% ad-free and privacy-first.

3. Third-Party Services & CDNs

To provide core functionality and visual design, we use the following external resources — none of which collect personal data about you:

  • Font Awesome Icons – loaded via Cloudflare CDN. Font Awesome’s privacy policy: https://fontawesome.com/privacy. Their CDN may collect anonymized request data (IP addresses are not retained).
  • Browser Web Speech API – all text-to-speech pronunciation is processed entirely inside your own browser (Google Chrome, Safari, Edge, etc.). No audio data is sent to any server. Zero external voice servers.
  • No Google Analytics, no Facebook Pixel, no Hotjar, no ad networks.

4. Children’s Privacy

NextGenLearningMyanmar is designed for learners of all ages, but we do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. Because we collect no personal data whatsoever, there is no risk of unauthorized data collection. Parents and guardians can rest assured that our website contains no user profiles, chats, or data storage features.

5. Your Rights (GDPR & Myanmar Privacy Standards)

Since we collect zero personal data, your rights are fully protected by default. You do not need to request data deletion, access, or correction — there is no data to manage. However, you can:

  • ✅ Clear your browser’s local storage / cookies at any time (removes cookie preferences).
  • ✅ Use incognito/private browsing mode to prevent any local storage from being saved.
  • ✅ Disable JavaScript entirely — the core word lists remain readable (though audio/search may not work).

6. Contact Us — Privacy Inquiries

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or data practices, please reach out to our designated contact:

📧 nextgenlearningmyanmar@gmail.com

We typically respond within 3–5 business days. For GDPR/legal privacy matters, please include "PRIVACY REQUEST" in the subject line.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally to reflect changes in technology or legal requirements. The most current version will always be posted on this page, with the "Last updated" date at the top. If we make any material change (e.g., introducing analytics), we will notify users via a visible banner on the homepage.

8. Hosting & Data Security

Our website is hosted on secure, reputable infrastructure. However, because we do not collect or store personal information, there is no database of user data that could be compromised. All interactions (searches, word views) happen client-side. We encourage you to keep your device and browser updated for maximum security.

Special note for Myanmar learners: Your privacy is your right. We designed NextGenLearningMyanmar to require no login, no tracking, and no data sharing — ever. You can learn English vocabulary freely without any digital footprint.
